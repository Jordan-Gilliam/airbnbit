  • Your name.
  • An email for us to respond to you.
  • How to contact you by telephone.
    (Preferably a cell with texting ability.)
  • Any comments you have.

Next, please tell us about the place you may offer as a host.
Here we need to know:

  • The address of the property.
  • A description of the property. Please include the tax id if you can - it would help us locate and evaluate some basic information on the property. Provide information on the number of bedrooms and bathrooms, approximate heated square feet, amenities at the property, neighborhood amenities, nearby amenities, and any other details that you think might be relevant.

When you submit this form, we will generate a unique ID for you. That will make it possible for you to simply log in later.
